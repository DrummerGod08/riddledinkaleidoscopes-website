The story

Riddled In Kaleidoscopes

Born from Philadelphia roots, Riddled In Kaleidoscopes blends emotional storytelling with the weight and energy of modern metal. R.I.K. is a solo-driven project built around music that turns struggle, survival, and growth into something fans can feel together.
